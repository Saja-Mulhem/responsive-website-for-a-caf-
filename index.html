Our Process

Nothing leaves the studio until it's right.

We've been asked why we don't automate more. The honest answer: because the variations are the point. The slight asymmetry in the rim. The place where your thumb rests. That's where the object becomes yours.

1

We gather materials

Clay sourced from the Pacific Northwest. Linen imported from Belgium. Dye plants grown or foraged locally.

2

We shape by hand

Thrown on the wheel or pinched by hand. Woven on floor looms. One piece at a time, every time.

3

We fire or finish

Ceramics fire at cone 6 — a slow, even heat that brings out the depth in each glaze. Textiles are washed and blocked by hand.

4

We inspect and ship

Every piece is checked before it's wrapped. Orders ship within 3–5 business days in recycled materials.
